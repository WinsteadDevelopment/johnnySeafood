Catering Your Next Boil

Our services

A One Stop Shop

Johnny's Seafood is fully outfitted to provide on-site catering services. We have our own tables, chairs, tents, and all the equipment to provide your next company event with delicious boiled seafood including crawfish, crabs, shrimp, and of course all the fixin's.

Disaster Relief

As strong believers in giving back to the community and helping those in need, we're committed to providing disaster relief services when it's needed. On short notice, we can feed anywhere from 1 to 1000 people three meals a day.

Corporate Events

Johnny's Seafood is proud to partner with local businesses both big and small. We can boil for any sized organization. To book us for your on-site catering needs, get in touch here.
